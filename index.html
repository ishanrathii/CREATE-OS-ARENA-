      ❓ FAQ

      When do predictions lock?

      Each match locks at the official kickoff time. You'll see a live countdown on the match card.

      What happens if a match is postponed?

      Your prediction carries to the rescheduled date. If the match is cancelled, the prediction is voided — no points won, no streak broken.

      How are favourite vs underdog decided?

      We use pre-match win probability. Anything below 35% is the underdog. We update this up to 1 hour before kickoff.

      Is this gambling?

      No. CreateOS Arena is a free, fun prediction game. No entry fees, no wagering, no cash payouts beyond the announced prize draws.
